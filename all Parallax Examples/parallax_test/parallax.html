Digital Cultures and Technologies

Started in 2010, the Digital Cultures and Technologies program covers both the social aspect of digital technologies as well as technical knowledge in interactive media.

Video Game Development

Students study video game writing and game development techniques, along with technical skills in Unity and C Sharp, as well as Blender.

Web Design

Students begin by learning HTML and CSS, and progress to Javascript, jQuery, and the MERN stack, as well as learning p5.js in the introductory class.
